# Everdell Base Game Card Colors/Categories

## Construction Cards

### Purple (Prosperity)
- Castle - unique (4) - 1 VP / common construction
- Ever Tree - unique (5) - 1 VP / prosperity card
- Palace - unique (4) - 1 VP / unique construction
- School - unique (2) - 1 VP / common critter
- Theatre - unique (3) - 1 VP / unique critter

### Red (Destination)
- Cemetery - unique (0) - Reveal 4 cards, play 1 free
- Chapel - unique (2) - Place 1pt + draw 2 cards / pt
- Inn - common (2) - Play meadow card for 3⬮ less
- Lookout - unique (2) - Copy basic or forest location
- Monastery - unique (1) - Give 2⬮ to gain 4 pts
- Post Office - common (2) - Give 2 cards, discard any, draw to limit
- Scurrbble Stadium - common (2) - [extra! extra! expansion]

### Blue (Governance)
- Clock Tower - unique (0) - 3 pts, at season pay 1 pt to take action
- Courthouse - unique (2) - Gain 1⬮, 1⬮, or 1⬮ after construction
- Crane - unique (1) - Discard to play construct for 3⬮ less
- Dungeon - unique (0) - Play card for 3⬮ less when critter locked
- University - unique (3) - Discard city card, refund, gain 1⬮ + 1pt

### Green (Production)
- Carnival - unique (2) - Draw 1 / green or gain 1⬮ / 2 green
- Fairgrounds - unique (3) - Draw 2 cards
- Farm - common (1) - Gain 1⬮
- General Store - common (1) - 1⬮ + 1⬮ if Farm
- Mine - common (2) - Gain 1⬮
- Resin Refinery - common (1) - Gain 1⬮
- Storehouse - common (2) - Place 3⬮, 2⬮, 1⬮, or 2⬮, take all ⬮
- Twig Barge - common (1) - Gain 2⬮

### Tan (Traveller)
- Ruins - common (0) - Discard city construction, refund + draw 2

## Critter Cards

### Purple (Prosperity)
- Architect - unique (2) - 1 VP / ⬮ and ⬮ to 6 VP max
- King - unique (4) - 1 VP / basic event + 2 VP / special event
- Scurrbble Champion - common (2) - [extra! extra! expansion]
- Wife - common (2) - 3 VP if Husband paired

### Red (Destination)
- Queen - unique (4) - Play card up to value 3 for free

### Blue (Governance)
- Historian - unique (1) - Draw 1 when critter or construction played
- Innkeeper - unique (1) - Discard to play critter for 3⬮ less
- Judge - unique (2) - Replace 1⬮ when critter or construction played
- Shopkeeper - unique (1) - Gain 1⬮ after critter played in your city

### Green (Production)
- Barge Toad - common (1) - Gain 2⬮ for each farm
- Chip Sweep - common (2) - Activate 1 production in your city
- Doctor - unique (4) - Pay up to 3⬮ to gain 1pt each
- Husband - common (2) - Gain 1⬮ if Wife paired and Farm
- Miner Mole - common (1) - Copy 1 production in oppo city
- Monk - unique (0) - Give up to 2⬮ to gain 2 pts each
- Peddler - common (1) - Pay up to 2⬮ to gain 1⬮ each
- Rugwort the Rowdy - unique (0) - [rugwort expansion]
- Teacher - common (2) - Draw 2, keep 1, give 1
- Town Crier - unique (2) - [extra! extra! expansion]
- Woodcarver - common (2) - Pay up to 3⬮ to gain 1pt each

### Tan (Traveller)
- Bard - unique (0) - Discard up to 5 cards, gain 1pt / card
- Fool - unique (-2) - Play to oppo city
- Postal Pigeon - common (0) - Reveal 2 cards, play 1 up to value 3 for free
- Ranger - unique (1) - Move worker, unlock 2nd cell
- Shepherd - unique (1) - Gain 3⬮ + 1 pt / Chapel pt
- Undertaker - unique (1) - Discard 3 meadow, replenish, draw 1
- Wanderer - common (1) - Draw 3 cards (DOES NOT COUNT TOWARD 15-CARD LIMIT)

## Special Rules
- Husband/Wife: When paired, share 1 space (count as 1 toward 15-card limit)
- Wanderer: Does NOT count toward 15-card limit (only exception in base game)
